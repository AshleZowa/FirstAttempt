I knew at that moment that my calling was in art. After high school, I attended the Johannesburg School of Arts, where I majored in photography. When I graduated, 5 years ago, I decided to be a freelance photographer. It has been an eventful experience that has made me who I am today.
Last year I started my own business and brand, World Of Photography and now the sky is the limit. Photography is my one true
love and my goal is to take you with me on this journey as we capture memorable moments that will last forever.
